The Others in Art

The Others in Art was a website created for hosting diffrent artist and showing off their artwork.

[Homepage]

The index page for The Others in Art was simple. It has a Navigation at the top with the site Logo. Under the Navigation Theres a slideshow with a couple of the artist work. Under the slideshow you can find headshots of all the artist as well as a bio for each of them.

[Artist]

When you click on Artist on the navigation, it takes you to a page where you can pick one of the four artist and look at the artwork.

[Artwork]

Once you select your artist you will be sent to a new page where you can get a better detailed description of the artist. Aswell you will be able to see their artwork with a description under.
